Wi-Fi Connection

Connect your Android device and PC to the same Wi-Fi network.

Alternatively, you can use your mobile phone’s hotspot to connect both devices if Wi-Fi is unavailable

Start SlideSync Server

Open the PC app, click "Start Server," and note the IP address displayed.

Connect to the PC Server

In the Android app, enter the IP address, then tap the connect button.

Check Status

Verify the connection status on both your phone and PC for confirmation.
